Our Profile

VKR is a renowned exporter and supplier of top-quality agro products, specializing in a wide range of fresh and authentic produce. Our offerings include fresh vegetables, premium Indian rice varieties like Basmati and non-Basmati, yellow maize, fresh pomegranates, and more. With a commitment to excellence, we ensure that all products are sourced from trusted manufacturers and skilled farmers, delivering quality you can rely on. Our Establishment Founded in 2021 and headquartered in Nashik, Maharashtra, India, VKR was established with the mission to transform the agro products industry. Although a young company, we have quickly gained recognition for our quality, service, and integrity. With two years of experience in the agro-export business, we are steadily carving out a prominent position in the market.
